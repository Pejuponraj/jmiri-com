About Us

Judah Mathematics & IT Research & Institution (JMIRI) is a center of academic and research excellence focused on Mathematical Sciences, Data Analytics, and Technological Innovation. We blend theoretical depth with practical insights to empower the next generation of researchers and professionals.

Programs Offered

  • Advanced Pure Mathematics
  • AI and Machine Learning with Python
  • Big Data Analytics & Power BI
  • Mathematical Modelling & Statistics
  • Workshops for School and College Students

Research Focus

Our research spans diverse areas including Algebra, Topology, Machine Learning, Number Theory, Data Visualization, and Educational Technology. We actively collaborate with international institutions and publish regularly in reputed journals.
